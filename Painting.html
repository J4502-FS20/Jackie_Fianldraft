The North Village Arts District will collectively open its doors on the first Friday of each month, encouraging residents, visitors, and tourists to explore and appreciate the ever-growing collections of art and entertainment that diversify and energize the community.

The First Friday provides urban professionals an opportunity to network, exchange, and share ideas on various art forms.

Orr Street Studio

Orr Street Studio is a non-profit art gallery located in the North Village artists District of Columbia, Missouri. Founded by Mark Timberlake in 2006, the space was established to provide an environment through which the community can interacr and participate directly with art and artists. It houses studio space and also contains a large gallery where they display rotsting exhibitions. Get more information at their website.

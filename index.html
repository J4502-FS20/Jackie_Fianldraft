Columbia is a city in the state of Missouri. It is the county seat of Boone County and home to the University of Missouri. As a Midwestern college town, Columbia has a reputation for progressive politics, persuasive journalism, and public art.

Music

Music festivals and live performance are always presenting in the city. Roots N Blues Festival are held annually in Columbia. "9th Street Summerfest" closes part of the street several nights each summer to hold outdoor performaces and has featured Willie Nelson. The University of Missouri School of Music also attracts hundreds of musicians to Columbia, student performances are held in Whitmore Recital Hall.

Painting

The North Village Arts District is a neighborhood and arts district in Columbia, Missouri. It is located on the north side of downtown Columbia, and is the city's main art gallery district and center for the visual arts. The neighborhood is also home to restaurants, bars, food trucks, housewares shops, theaters, and a distillery.

Film

Living in Columbia, you can easily enjoy the beauty of film. The city holds various film festivals and has two commercial cinemas and a non-profit Cinema. True/False Film Festival is an anuual documentary film festival that takes place on the first weekend in March, with films being shown from Thursday evening to Sunday night.
